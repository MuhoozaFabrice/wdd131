South Kivu, Democratic Republic of Congo

South Kivu is a province in the eastern part of the Democratic Republic of Congo, located along the shores of Lake Kivu and bordering Rwanda and Burundi. Its capital city is Bukavu, known for its scenic hills and views of Lake Kivu. The region is rich in natural resources, diverse cultures, and stunning landscapes, including parts of Kahuzi-Biega National Park, home to endangered eastern lowland gorillas.
